All About CompuTerps

CompuTerps is a student led tech club at University of Maryland that is focused on helping people gain a deeper knowledge of the technology that is around them everyday. At our meetings we spend time discussing new developments in the computer industry, such as hardware and software, as well as teaching and providing resources for concepts that are new to our members.

Anyone who is a student at the University of Maryland is welcome to join.

No tech experience necessary!

You can register via TerpLink, come to one of our meetings, or email us here
