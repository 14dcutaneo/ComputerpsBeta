About

What is CompuTerps?

 

CompuTerps is a student led tech club at University of Maryland that is focused on helping people gain a deeper knowledge of the technology that is around them everyday. At our meetings we spend time discussing new developments in the computer industry, such as hardware and software, as well as providing methods of exploring further.

 

 

Who can join CompuTerps?

 

Anyone who is a student at the University of Maryland is welcome to join.

No tech experience necessary

 

 

How do I join?

 

You can register via TerpLink, come to one of our meetings, contact us at
